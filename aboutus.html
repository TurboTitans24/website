POLICIES & TERMS

Our commitment to transparency and ethical operations is codified in these foundational policies.

Engagement Terms

+

1. Project Initiation: All engagements begin with a discovery phase to align objectives, scope, and success metrics. A formal Statement of Work (SOW) must be signed before commencement.

2. Payment Structure: We operate on milestone-based payments with 30% upfront for new clients. Final deliverables are released upon full payment.

3. Intellectual Property: Unless otherwise agreed, clients retain full ownership of deliverables. Turbo Titans reserves the right to showcase work in our portfolio (confidential projects may be anonymized).

4. Termination: Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. Client remains responsible for payments for work completed up to termination date.

Security Policy

+

Turbo Titans adheres to the highest security standards to protect client data and systems:

  • All team members undergo rigorous background checks and security training
  • End-to-end encryption for all communications and file transfers
  • Multi-factor authentication required for all systems access
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant data protection regulations
  • Immediate notification protocol for any security incidents

Confidentiality Agreement

+

1. Non-Disclosure: All client information is treated as confidential unless explicitly designated as public. This obligation continues indefinitely beyond project completion.

2. Need-to-Know Basis: Information is shared only with team members directly involved in the project.

3. Data Handling: Client data is stored in encrypted formats with strict access controls. Physical media is prohibited unless explicitly authorized.

4. Anonymization: Case studies may reference client work only with permission or when sufficiently anonymized.

Service Level Agreement

+

Turbo Titans commits to the following service standards:

  • Response Times: 2 business hours for critical issues, 8 hours for high priority, 24 hours for standard inquiries
  • Uptime: 99.9% for managed services and platforms
  • Support Coverage: 24/7 monitoring and emergency support for designated clients
  • Change Management: All changes follow documented approval and rollback procedures
  • Performance Metrics: Monthly reports detailing SLA compliance and service quality

Ethical Framework

+

We uphold strict ethical standards in all engagements:

  • We refuse projects involving surveillance technology, offensive cyber operations, or technologies that may cause societal harm
  • All AI implementations include bias testing and ethical impact assessments
  • Transparent algorithms - no "black box" solutions without client understanding
  • Environmental impact considered in all technology recommendations
  • Whistleblower protection for team members reporting ethical concerns
